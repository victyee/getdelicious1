Getting Started

Accessing features and functions

All features and functions are accessed through the dropdown menu on the top right hand corner. Here you can setup your food truck's profile and manage your food packages.

Your food truck's profile

This is the first thing you'll need to setup. Just follow through collumns and fill in the required fields. After you've completed the profile, you may realise that your profile is not live yet. This is because you need to add at least 1 food package.

Adding food packages

Next you'll start creating food packages. You can create as many as you like, so take you time and be creative with your packages! Once you've added a package, you'll see your food truck live!

What's this 'service hours' and 'kms'?

We make it easy for you to include additional costs if required.

Included Service Hours: You just need to put the number of food service hours included in the package. In general, 2 hours should be sufficient.

Included Travelling: Similarly, just enter the amount of included kilometers you're willing to travel from the CBD.

Additional Service Hours: In the event that additional hours are required, just set the rate of how much you'll charge per additional hour.

Additional Travelling: Here you'll enter the additional charge per km that you'll need to travel to the client's venue.

Accepting Jobs

When a customer books you for a job, you'll be notified via email. You'll have up to 48 hours to accept the booking. If you have any queries for the customer, you can reply directly on the email.

Fees and Payments

Fees

There are no fees for being listed on getdelicious. We take a percentage off each confirmed job booked through getdelicious

For each job, we take a 8% fee + 2% transaction fee.

Payments

All payments to you will be via bank transfer.

Once you've accepted a booking, we will transfer a 20% non refundable deposit to your nominated bank account. The balance will be transferred 1 day after the event ends. If the job get cancelled, you get to keep the 20% non refundable deposit.

Can't find what you're looking for?

Not a problem. Just email us at hungry@getdelicious.com.au
