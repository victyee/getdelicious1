FAQ

  • Frequently asked questions

1. What is getdelicious?

getdelicious is the place to book delicious food trucks for your event!

2. How does it work?

Easy!

  • Start by selecting your state.
  • Select a food truck.
  • Checkout the menus, prices and availabilities.
  • Enter your details.
  • And done!.

3. How does payment work?

Once you submit a booking request, we'll place a hold on your credit card. You'll NOT be charged until the food truck operator accepts your booking. Allow 1 to 2 days.

4. How do I know my booking has been confirmed?

It usually take 1 to 2 day for the food truck operator to accpet booking requests. Once accepted, you will receive a confirmation email. You will NOT be charged until the food truck operator accepts your booking

5. I've got a change of plan

We understand sometimes things don't go according to plan, and you might need to make changes. At this point, it is best to speak directly to the food truck operators. Depending on the situation, they may accommodate changes at their discretion.

6. Can I change my order details after confirmation?

Just shoot us an email. You can finalise guest numbers a week before the event's date - number of guests cannot fall below the minimum required. Other changes (such as dates, service hours, etc) are STRICTLY subject to the operator.

7. Refund / Cancellation Policy?

We'll refund 70% of the payment if cancellations are made after booking confirmation. No refund if cancellation is made within 1 week of the event's date.

8. How do I get my food truck listed on the website?

Simply signup and make sure you tick the 'food truck owner' box

9. Nothing is showing up in my state

Bummer, check back later or send us your requirements and we'll help you out!

10. How do I fill in additional service hours and additional kms?

All packages includes a certain amount of service hours and travels in kilometers. You only need to add extra hours you need in addition to what is provided. Same goes with extra kms. If your venue is further than what is included, just enter the extra amount.
